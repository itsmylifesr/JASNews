Update: Program ECHO

Program ECHO is a strategic initiative that seeks to design, standardize and implement new JAS Global milestones to increase operational efficiency and ensure compliance. The program integrates a suit of projects such as RODEO, SONIC, COMANDO! and MISURA, aiming to improve data quality, optimize processes and enhance the customer experience through tools such as JAS SmartHub.

Key Objectives

• Align JAS Standards with Market Expectations

Elevate standards to meet and exceed client demands.

• Enhance Customer-Facing Digital Capabilities

Implement solutions such as standard reporting packs, delay management and reporting, and customer transit time reporting.

• Optimize Service Performance and Quality through Automation

Leverage automation to improve milestone tracking and reporting, vendor performance management, and provide digital desk reference tools for operators.

  • ECHO Standards (IT):

The IT component is fundamental to managing and implementing tasks across SmartHub, C1 and other integrated systems. Project milestones will be developed in three priority phases:

• Priority 1: Milestones with minimal dependencies, currently in progress.
• Priority 2: Milestones with moderate dependencies, which will be addressed next.
• Priority 3: Milestones with complex interdependencies, addressed in the last phase.

Workflow clean-ups are currently underway to support the integration of Priority 1 milestones, with testing being planned to begin in select smaller countries, pending final confirmations. Additionally, the redesign of milestone layouts in SmartHub is ongoing, with further updates to milestone icons and continued testing.

  • Program ECHO – and its Projects:

RODEO: Enhance how we report Transit Time and Delays with Delay Reason Codes.

COMANDO!: Transform how we make information available to our teams with our “Forwarding Instructions Portal”

SONIC: Reporting and Analytics on Steroids, powered by SmartHub!

MISURA: Measuring our Vendors on their Automation and Reporting linked to it.

BENJI Update: Pricing Solution

Implement automated, real-time dynamic pricing for quoting and booking, serving both internal and external customers. The project covers Spot, Contract and Traffic Quotes for Air and Ocean products, with advanced rate generation capabilities for RFQs.

Project BENJI continues to reach new milestones, most notably the launch of Phase Two – Contract Quote and Book for our four pilot customers. We are working to expand this innovative solution, onboarding even more customers and raising the bar for efficiency in the quoting and booking process.

Spotlight: Document Automation – CIV + PKL

In freight forwarding, one of the most time-consuming activities and manual processes is to capture data from our customers’ documents and convert it into our platform to be used in our current processes and documents.

To help our operators to be more productive and to focus on other activities that drive more value in our process, we have developed the Document Automation solution for Commercial Invoices and Packing Lists (CIV + PKL)!

Our tool uses edge Optical Character Recognition (OCR) solutions to read the documents received from our customers in several formats. Our flow will then identify the CIV and PKL from the documents received and use their data for further analysis of Artificial Intelligence (AI). A prompt is used to identify in the document main data points and an Electronic Connection update these to our CargoWiseOne (C1) system.

Overall process flow:

• Send E-mail

• Include Shipment # or Hbl #

• Subject or E-mail Body

Magic Happens

• Identify CIV & PKL
• Data
• Incoterm
• Goods Value & Currency
• References
• Updates C1 Fields
